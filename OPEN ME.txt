ENIGMA — an interactive cipher tutorial
=======================================

TO OPEN IT:

  Double-click  index.html

It opens in your normal web browser. There is nothing to install.

Then just scroll down the page and play with each section — turn the
Caesar wheel, type on the keyboard, watch the lamps light up. By the
end you'll be typing on a full working Enigma machine.

Tip: connect to the internet the first time you open it, so the page
loads its proper fonts and looks its best. It still works offline.

Enjoy!
